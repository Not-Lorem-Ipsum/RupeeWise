What is the purpose of RupeeWise? +
RupeeWise helps you manage your finances by providing features like budgeting, expense tracking, investment advice, and bill payment reminders.
Can this bot help me track my expenses? +
Absolutely! The bot categorizes your transactions and provides insights into your spending habits.
Does the bot support multiple currencies? +
Yes, the bot supports multiple currencies and can provide a consolidated view of your finances in your preferred currency.
Can I set savings goals with this bot? +
Yes, you can set savings goals, and the bot will help you track your progress and provide tips to stay on track.
Is the RupeeWise free to use? +
Currently the RupeeWise is free to use and all the features is available to everyone.
